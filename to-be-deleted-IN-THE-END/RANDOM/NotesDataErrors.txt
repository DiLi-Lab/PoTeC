SR Research (Brian):
Sorry I did forget to mention a step for fixing up the problem such as is seen in session 10. For session 10 (and probably many others) where there are only a few out of the 7 total interest areas being shown for each screen, you will have to uncheck the option for collapsing the interest areas, save the preferences (right click in the top preferences window, and select "save as default"), then start a new Data Viewer session and re-import all your data. The collapsing step happens at Data Loading time, and so the .edf files have to be re-imported in a new session once the setting is changed. My apologies I did not mention that before.
